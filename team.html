Thrisha Kalpatthi

Co-Founder

Thrisha Kalpatthi is a first-year BS/MD student at Penn State University. She is a four-time AIME Qualifier, has attended National Mathcounts, National Science Bowl, and the USA Brain Bee, and completed AP Calculus BC in 8th grade. She is involved with PSU Natya, the collegiate Indian Classical Dance team and loves to dance in her free time. She also enjoys hanging out with friends and family, usually spending her time watching movies!

Havisha's passion for science is evident through her neuroscience research and her leadership of a neuroscience club in Pittsburgh. She also placed 6th at the USA Brain Bee.
